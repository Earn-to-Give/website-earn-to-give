About Earn to Give

Earn to Give's mission is to enhance the way that people give to charities online while improving the financial literacy of America.

We aim to achieve our mission by offering two core products - an online fundraising platform and financial literacy coaching.

Offerings In Development

  • Giving Guide™
  • Giving Profile
  • Non-profit Fundraising Platform
  • Gift Your Influence™
  • Financial Literacy Coaching
